## Writing Posts

Create new posts in the `_posts` directory. Name them using the format:

```
YYYY-MM-DD-title-slug.md
```

Each post needs front matter at the top:

```yaml
---
layout: post
title: "Your Post Title"
date: YYYY-MM-DD
---
```

Then write your content in Markdown below.
