Water Pouring Problem Simulation

You have two cups. You want to obtain a given target volume of water. You can:

  • Pour water from one cup to another
  • Empty one of the cups
  • Fill up one of the cups with water

How do you obtain final target volume? This problem is a good introduction to the idea of breadth first search (BFS). This animation is intended to make the idea of BFS easier to grasp via an animating example. Click Simulate to begin!
